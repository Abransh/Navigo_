Talk to Gliss
How is Gliss different from other virtual women's health companies?

You're probably comparing us to other health service companies and we love how proactive and solution-oriented you are about your own health. There's a lot out there to explore, but here is how Gliss is different:

  1. The Gliss medical team are all board-certified OB/GYNs. We aren't life coaches or menopause coaches. We are physicians with expertise in and passion for supporting women through perimenopause and menopause. We are licensed to practice in all 50 states, DC and Guam and can prescribe most medications in most states.
  2. We do prescribe medications, including HRT. You can choose to have your prescriptions filled at your preferred pharmacy. We can also ship some medications to you in the mail.
  3. There is no general membership at Gliss. We offer service packages based on your unique needs, so you don't pay for what you don't need. Our prices don't include medication, since you'll be filling your scripts at your local pharmacy, where you can use your health insurance plan for added coverage.
  4. We are an extension of your brick-and-mortar OB/GYN or general physician. Since we offer virtual-only care, we can't do physical exams. But we can order labs, tests and medications. Plus, we give more one-on-one time to listen to all your concerns and review your health history, current medications, and previous tests. We can then make diagnoses and create treatment and support plans that complement your traditional onsite care.
Is Gliss Wellness an app?

Great question! As a Gliss Woman, you'll get an invitation to download the Healthie app. Healthie is a HIPAA-compliant medical record that connects clients and providers for on-the-go care. This is your private patient portal, where you can schedule your appointment, video-call and message with your doctor, share your health records including history, lab work and scans, and more.

Do you only help women during perimenopause / menopause?

Thanks for asking! As board-certified OB/GYNs, we help women of all ages from teens all the way to post-menopause. We can provide STI treatments, birth control, safe sex counseling and prescriptions based on each woman's needs and body. This includes counseling on how to use emergency contraception.

Since we are exclusively virtual, we don't provide care during pregnancy.

How does the first virtual consultation work?

What can we talk about in 15 minutes? A lot! This short session is our opportunity to meet each other and figure out if this patient-doctor relationship feels like a good fit. We'll start by asking what kind of experiences have led you to reach out to Gliss and you can ask us anything you want!

Then we'll run through the kinds and costs of services that we can help with. By the end of our 15 minutes together, you'll have a really great sense of who we are, how we'll help, how much it costs and how to get started with Gliss.

This initial consultation is private and secure, and it's totally up to you how much you share with us on this call. We won't diagnose or prescribe any treatment plans or medications.

We want you to feel comfortable making such a big personal decision, so we're offering the initial consultation call for free. We won't even make you fill out endless questionnaires or online forms to book it. All we need is the usual basic contact info, and you're good to go!
